Privacy Policy
Last updated: February 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Shohrux Umarov, operating under the name Level Infinity, collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Hamyon mobile application ("the App").

1. Information We Collect

Hamyon collects only what is strictly necessary to provide expense tracking:

  • Expense records — amount, category, date, and optional comment that you enter manually.
  • Anonymous device ID (UUID) — a randomly generated identifier created on first launch. It is not linked to your name, email, or any personal identity.
  • Daily budget limit — stored only on your device.
  • Language preference — stored only on your device.
No name or email collected No location data No contacts or camera No advertising ID

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To save and display your expense history.
  • To sync your expenses between sessions using your anonymous UUID.
  • To calculate and show daily spending statistics and budget status.

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, profiling, or any purpose beyond the App's core functionality.

3. Data Storage

On your device
Expenses and settings are stored locally using Hive encrypted database.

On our server
Expense data is synced to hamyon.nexon.uz — a server maintained solely by the developer, located in Uzbekistan. No other parties have access to this server.

4. Third-Party Services

No analytics (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.) No advertising SDKs No social login No crash reporting services

No data is shared with or sold to any third party.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained as long as you use the App.

  • Local data: Uninstalling the App permanently removes all locally stored data.
  • Server data: Contact us using the email below to request deletion. We will permanently remove all data associated with your UUID within 7 business days.

6. Children's Privacy

Hamyon is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
